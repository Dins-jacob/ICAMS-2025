About The Conference

St. Dominic's College Kanjirapally, a beacon of academic excellence in Kerala, invites you to join us for ICAMS-2025 (International Conference on Advances in Material Science 2025), a landmark conference celebrating its 60th anniversary. From nanotechnology to biomaterials, ICAMS-2025 serves as a platform to showcase the latest breakthroughs in material science and offers a unique opportunity to explore the cutting-edge research that is shaping our future. By uniting a diverse community of world's leading material scientists, researchers, scholars, and students from around the globe, ICAMS-2025 creates a fertile ground for the exploration of cutting-edge research, the sharing of innovative ideas, and the establishment of lasting partnerships. In addition, the conference features unique and exclusive tours that include the breathtaking hill stations, serene backwaters, and pristine beaches of Kerala - "the God's Own Country". The cultural night and reception showcasing Kerala's distinct culture, cuisine, and warmth add to the grandeur of the conference, making it an unparalleled experience.

About The College

St. Dominic's College, Kanjirapally, run by the Eparchy of Kanjirapally, stands tall with its 60 years of proud legacy in the realm of higher education. The verdant campus is ensconced in the serene foothills of the Western Ghats, a UNESCO designated World Heritage site. Many renowned personalities, having left an indelible mark in science, arts, literature, social, and political spheres, both nationally and internationally, are distinguished alumni of this esteemed institution. The college has been ranked among the top 150 Indian colleges by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) and all the Science departments are supported by DST-FIST. The college offeres 13 undergraduate and 6 postgraduate programs and is situated 40 kms away from Kottayam (KTYM) railway station and 97 kms away from Cochin International airport (COK).

Call for papers

The broad topics to be covered, but not limited to, will be the following:

    • 2D-Materials and Nanocomposites
    • Advanced and Smart Materials
    • Characterization Tools
    • Building Materials Engineering
    • Material Science Engineering
    • Computation and Simulations in Material Science
    • Crystal Growth and Applications
    • Energy Materials
    • Energy Storage and Harvesting Materials
    • Environmental, Sustainable, and Green Materials
    • Experimental & Functional Materials
    • Glasses, Thin Films and Ultrasonics
    • Indian Vision on Material Science
    • Inorganic Compounds
    • Luminescence
    • Magnetic & Dielectric Materials
    • Materials for Bio-medical Applications
    • Nanocomposites
    • Nonlinear Optics
    • Phytotechnology and Phytochemistry

All accepted papers will be published in any one of the following Scopus Indexed conference proceedings. The authors should pay a nominal APC (Article Processing Charge) apart from the registration fee.

  • 1. Springer Nature
  • 2. Journal of Physics: Conference Series (Proposal Submitted)
  • 3. AIP Conference Proceedings (Proposal Submitted)
