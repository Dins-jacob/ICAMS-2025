About The Conference

ICAMS-2025 will be one of the immense national events exclusively dedicated to advances in Science and Technology Research. The conference will be extremely useful in the wisdom of interdisciplinary gathering that it will be attended by hundreds of physicists, chemists, technologists and engineers. The goal of the conference is to bring the advanced research on sciences and Technology on a knowledge platform to facilitate the researchers to interact with a panel of highly-accomplished experts in the fields. Talks will encompass basic studies, applications and will address topics of novel issues. In the two-day event, we will listen to recognized personalities in the field as they discuss recent advances, difficulties, and breakthroughs in the fields.

Keynote address, invited talks, contributory papers poster, oral presentation and an exhibition of books, equipment and other related materials have been planned. In the conference latest developments in the proposed fields will be reviewed. The conference is also expected to provide a platform to share their experiences with the delegates and scientists for future collaborations.

St. Dominic's College, Kanjirapally
St. Dominic's College, Kanjirapally, instituted by the great visionaries of the Eparchy of Kanjirapally, stands tall with its 60 years of proud legacy in the realm of higher education. The verdant campus is ensconced in the serene foothills of the Western Ghats, a UNESCO designated World Heritage site. The abundant tapestry of a rich academic culture and the contributions of a highly literate community with seamless virtues here offers the best backdrop for profound academic engagement and insightful research. The college has been ranked among the top 150 Indian colleges by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) and all the Science departments are supported by DST-FIST. The college has 13 undergraduate and 6 postgraduate programs. The College is situated 40 kms away from Kottayam (KTYM) railway station and 100 kms away from Cochin International airport.
